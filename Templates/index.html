About Our Food

For over 40 years, we've been preparing biryani with our secret recipe passed down through generations. All spices and herbs are handpicked, natural, with no added preservatives. Join us at our biryani haven and embark on a culinary adventure like no other. Whether you're dining in with friends and family or opting for a takeaway treat, let us whisk you away on a journey of taste, tradition, and pure gastronomic bliss.
